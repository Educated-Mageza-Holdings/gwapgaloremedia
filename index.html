Training Essentials

Start Slow

Begin with a comfortable pace and gradually increase your distance and speed. Listen to your body and avoid overtraining.

Proper Form

Maintain good posture, land mid-foot, keep your arms relaxed, and breathe rhythmically for optimal performance.

Rest & Recovery

Allow your body time to recover between runs. Rest days are just as important as training days for preventing injury.

Stay Hydrated

Drink water before, during, and after your runs. Proper hydration is crucial for performance and recovery.
